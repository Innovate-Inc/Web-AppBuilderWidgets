Build your first app

To build a basic web app, complete the following steps:

  1. Start Web AppBuilder.
  2. Log in to your portal (ArcGIS Online Organization or Portal for ArcGIS).
  3. On the Web AppBuilder home page, click Create New.
  4. Provide the app name and description, and click OK.
  5. Accept the default theme, map, and widgets.
  6. Click the Attributes tab, change the title, subtitle, and logo of the app.
  7. Click Save to save your changes.
  8. Click Previews to view the app in devices.
  9. Click Launch to view the app in the browser.
  10. Click the Web AppBuilder icon or Web AppBuilder for ArcGIS at the top left of the browser to return to the home page.
  11. Click the More icon , and click Download on the menu to download the app for deployment.
